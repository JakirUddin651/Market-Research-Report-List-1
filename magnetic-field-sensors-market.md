<p><p>The magnetic field sensors market encompasses various technologies, including Hall Effect sensors, which detect magnetic fields using semiconductor materials; AMR (Anisotropic Magnetoresistance) sensors, which are sensitive to magnetic field direction and strength; and GMR (Giant Magnetoresistance) sensors, known for their high sensitivity and used in advanced applications. Other types include fluxgate, inductive, and magneto-resistive sensors. Each type serves different applications, from automotive and industrial to consumer electronics, contributing to the growing demand for precise magnetic field measurement.</p></p>
