ATJ-SPK (Alcohol-to-Jet Synthetic Paraffinic Kerosene) converts alcohols into jet fuel. Other categories include emerging technologies and feedstocks. Each type plays a crucial role in diversifying fuel sources and reducing carbon emissions in aviation.</p></p>
