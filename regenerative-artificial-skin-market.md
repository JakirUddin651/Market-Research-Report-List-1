<p><p>The regenerative artificial skin market is categorized into three main types: temporary skin material, permanent skin material, and composite skin material. Temporary skin materials are designed for short-term coverage of wounds, promoting healing while allowing fluid exchange. Permanent skin materials aim for long-lasting solutions, integrating with the patient's skin over time. Composite skin materials combine both temporary and permanent functionalities, offering initial protection and then encouraging long-term regeneration. Each type plays a crucial role in advancing wound care and tissue engineering.</p></p>
