<p><strong>The Range Extenders Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single Band (2.4 GHz) Range Extenders</li><li>Dual Band (2.4 GHz+5 GHz) Range Extenders</li><li>Tri-Band (2.4 GHz+5 GHz+5 GHz) Range Extenders</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Range Extenders market features three main types: Single Band (2.4 GHz) Range Extenders, which amplify signals but only operate on the 2.4 GHz frequency, providing basic coverage. Dual Band (2.4 GHz + 5 GHz) Range Extenders utilize both frequencies to offer improved performance and reduced congestion, making them ideal for mixed-device environments. Tri-Band (2.4 GHz + 5 GHz + 5 GHz) Range Extenders add an extra 5 GHz band, optimizing speed and connectivity for high-demand applications and multiple users simultaneously.</p></p>
