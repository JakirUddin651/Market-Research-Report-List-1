<p><p>The Anti-Lock Braking System (ABS) market encompasses three primary components: the Electronic Control Unit (ECU), sensors, and the hydraulic unit. The ECU serves as the system's brain, processing data from sensors to regulate brake pressure and prevent wheel lock-up during hard braking. Sensors detect wheel speed and provide real-time feedback to the ECU. The hydraulic unit manages brake fluid pressure, adjusting it as needed to maintain optimal braking performance. Together, these components enhance vehicle safety and control.</p></p>
