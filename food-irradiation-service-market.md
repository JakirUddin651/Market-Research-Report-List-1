<p><p>The food irradiation service market encompasses three primary types of radiation: Electron Beam Radiation, Gamma Radiation, and X-Ray Radiation. Electron Beam Radiation utilizes high-energy electrons to rapidly irradiate food, ensuring quick processing and minimal heat impact. Gamma Radiation employs radioactive isotopes to penetrate food, effectively killing pathogens and extending shelf life. X-Ray Radiation combines characteristics of both, providing deep penetration and enhanced quality preservation. Each method enhances food safety and longevity while maintaining nutritional value, catering to diverse industry needs.</p></p>
