<p><strong>The Respiratory Protection Equipment(RPE) Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Negative Pressure APR</li><li>Positive Pressure PAPR</li><li>Self-Contained Breathing Apparatus (SCBA)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Respiratory Protection Equipment (RPE) encompasses various types designed to safeguard users from airborne hazards. Negative Pressure Air-Purifying Respirators (APR) filter contaminants as users inhale, relying on a tight fit for effectiveness. Positive Pressure Powered Air-Purifying Respirators (PAPR) use a battery-powered fan to supply filtered air, offering a higher level of protection and comfort. Self-Contained Breathing Apparatus (SCBA) provides breathable air from a tank, suitable for environments with no available oxygen, commonly used in emergency situations and industrial applications.</p></p>
