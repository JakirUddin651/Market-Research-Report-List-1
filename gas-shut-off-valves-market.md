<p><strong>The Gas Shut-off Valves Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Motorised Valve</li><li>Solenoid Valve</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The gas shut-off valves market includes motorised and solenoid valves, which serve critical safety and control functions in gas distribution systems. Motorised valves utilize electric actuators to regulate gas flow automatically, ensuring precise control and responsiveness to system demands. In contrast, solenoid valves operate using electromagnetic coils to open or close, providing quick and reliable actuation for gas shut-off during emergencies. Both types are essential for safeguarding against leaks and ensuring safe operation in various industrial and residential applications.</p></p>
