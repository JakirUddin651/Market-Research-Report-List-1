<p><strong>The Precious Metal Flotation Reagents Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Collector</li><li>Activator</li><li>Inhibitor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The precious metal flotation reagents market comprises three main types: collectors, activators, and inhibitors. Collectors are chemicals that enhance the hydrophobic properties of precious metal particles, promoting their attachment to air bubbles during flotation. Activators assist in increasing the effectiveness of collectors by modifying the surface properties of minerals. Inhibitors, on the other hand, prevent certain unwanted minerals from attaching to bubbles, ensuring a more selective extraction of precious metals. Together, these reagents optimize recovery processes in mining.</p></p>
