<p><p>Active Noise Cancellation (ANC) headphones are classified into three main types: In-Ear, On-Ear, and Over-Ear. In-Ear ANC headphones fit snugly inside the ear canal, providing portability and passive noise isolation. On-Ear headphones rest on the ears, offering a balance between comfort and sound quality, while still being relatively compact. Over-Ear headphones encase the entire ear, delivering superior sound quality and noise cancellation, making them ideal for immersive listening experiences. Each type caters to different user preferences and lifestyles.</p></p>
