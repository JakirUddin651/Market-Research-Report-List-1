<p><p>Thin-film RTD (Resistance Temperature Detector) temperature sensors are precision devices used for accurate temperature measurements in various industrial and consumer applications. These sensors utilize a thin layer of metal, typically platinum, that is deposited onto a substrate, allowing for rapid thermal response and high accuracy.
