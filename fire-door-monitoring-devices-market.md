<p><p>Fire door monitoring devices are essential for ensuring the proper functioning of fire safety systems. Normally Open Fire Door Monitoring Systems maintain doors in an open position under standard conditions, automatically closing during a fire alarm. In contrast, Normally Closed Fire Door Monitoring Systems keep doors closed unless triggered to open by specific events. These systems play a crucial role in maintaining safety by preventing fire spread while facilitating safe egress during emergencies, thus enhancing overall fire protection measures.</p></p>
