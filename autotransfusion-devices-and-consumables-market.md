<p><p>The autotransfusion devices and consumables market encompasses various products used to collect and reinfuse a patient's blood during surgery. Device types include on-pump transfusion devices, which operate in conjunction with cardiopulmonary bypass, allowing for the recovery and reinfusion of blood during heart surgeries. Off-pump transfusion devices, on the other hand, function independently of such support systems, facilitating blood retrieval in other surgical settings. Consumables include the necessary accessories such as blood collection bags and filters essential for the autotransfusion process.</p></p>
