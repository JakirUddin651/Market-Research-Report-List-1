<p><strong>The OLED Cellphone Display Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>PMOLED</li><li>AMOLED</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The OLED cellphone display market primarily consists of two types: PMOLED (Passive Matrix OLED) and AMOLED (Active Matrix OLED). PMOLED is simpler and less expensive, ideal for smaller screens with lower resolution, making it suitable for basic applications. In contrast, AMOLED features individual pixel control, allowing for higher resolutions, better color reproduction, and faster refresh rates. This makes AMOLED the preferred choice for high-end smartphones, delivering vibrant visuals and energy efficiency, which drives its dominance in the premium segment of the market.</p></p>
