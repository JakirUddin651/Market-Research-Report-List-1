<p><ul><li>Contact Profilometer</li><li>Non-Contact Profilometer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The profilometers market is primarily divided into two types: contact and non-contact profilometers. Contact profilometers utilize a stylus that physically touches the surface to measure its profile and roughness, providing accurate data for materials with various textures. In contrast, non-contact profilometers employ optical methods, such as lasers or light, to capture surface measurements without direct contact, making them ideal for sensitive materials or intricate geometries. Both types serve critical roles in industries such as manufacturing, aerospace, and electronics.</p></p>
