<p><p>Dry screed floor panels are an innovative flooring solution designed to provide a level, stable base for various flooring finishes without the need for traditional wet screed applications. These panels are typically made from gypsum or cement-bonded materials, offering benefits such as quick installation, reduced drying time, and improved thermal efficiency. Their lightweight design allows for easy handling and can help speed up the construction process, making them a preferred choice for both residential and commercial projects.</p><p>The Dry Screed Floor Panels Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 10% during the forecast period.
