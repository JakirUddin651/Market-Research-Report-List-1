<p><p>The hydro-processing catalysts market is primarily divided into two segments: hydrotreating and hydrocracking. Hydrotreating catalysts are used to remove impurities such as sulfur and nitrogen from petroleum fractions, enhancing product quality and meeting regulatory standards. On the other hand, hydrocracking catalysts facilitate the conversion of heavy feedstocks into lighter, more valuable products like diesel and jet fuel through the application of high pressure and temperature. Both processes are essential for refining operations and optimizing output efficiency.</p></p>
