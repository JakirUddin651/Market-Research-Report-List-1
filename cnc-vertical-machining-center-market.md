<p><p>The CNC Vertical Machining Center market includes various types of machines based on their axes of movement. A 2-axis CNC machine operates on two perpendicular axes, suitable for simple tasks. The 3-axis machine adds a third axis, enabling more complex shapes. A 4-axis machine introduces rotational movement around one axis, enhancing production capabilities. The 5-axis machine allows for full movement in five different directions, facilitating intricate designs and reducing setup time, making it ideal for high-precision applications.</p></p>
