<p><strong>The Rotary Drilling Bits Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Roller-Cone Bits</li><li>Fixed-Cutter Bits</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The rotary drilling bits market is primarily divided into two types: roller-cone bits and fixed-cutter bits. Roller-cone bits feature rotating cones that crush rock formations, making them ideal for various applications, including oil and gas extraction. Fixed-cutter bits, on the other hand, use a series of fixed cutting edges to shear the rock, offering advantages in soft formations and requiring less maintenance. Both types cater to different drilling environments and play a crucial role in enhancing drilling efficiency and effectiveness.</p></p>
