<p><p>The lactic acid market is segmented into three main types: L-lactic acid, D-lactic acid, and DL-lactic acid. L-lactic acid is primarily used in food, pharmaceuticals, and biodegradable plastics due to its natural origin and compatibility with human metabolism. D-lactic acid finds applications in some specialty chemicals and is important in certain microbial processes. DL-lactic acid, a racemic mixture, is often used in research and industrial applications. Each type serves distinct sectors based on their properties and uses.</p></p>
