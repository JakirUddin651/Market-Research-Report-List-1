<p><p>A Bipolar Electrosurgical Generator is a medical device used in surgical procedures that employs high-frequency electrical currents to cut tissue or coagulate blood. This technology is increasingly favored in minimally invasive surgeries.</p><p>The Bipolar Electrosurgical Generator Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 9.9% during the forecast period.
