<p><p>The green rebar market encompasses various types of reinforcing bars, primarily deformed steel and mild steel. Deformed steel rebars, characterized by their ridged surface, provide superior grip and bonding with concrete, making them ideal for high-strength applications. In contrast, mild steel rebars, known for their ductility and weldability, are used in less demanding constructions. Both types cater to diverse construction needs, focusing on sustainability and reduced carbon footprints by incorporating eco-friendly production methods and recycled materials.</p></p>
