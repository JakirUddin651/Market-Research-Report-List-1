<p><p>The Endoscopic Ultrasound (EUS) needles market is primarily categorized into two types: aspiration needles (EUS-FNA) and biopsy needles (EUS-FNB). EUS-FNA is commonly used for obtaining cytological samples from lesions, providing quick diagnosis for conditions like cancer. In contrast, EUS-FNB involves larger gauge needles that allow for core biopsies, yielding histological samples for more detailed analysis. Both needle types are essential for minimally invasive procedures in gastrointestinal diagnostics, enhancing the accuracy of disease characterization and treatment planning.</p></p>
