<p><p>The Photovoltaic PVDF resin market is primarily segmented into two types: PVDF granules and PVDF powder. PVDF granules are solid, pellet-like forms that are often used in extrusion processes, providing improved flow and processing characteristics for various applications. In contrast, PVDF powder is a fine particulate form that allows for better surface coverage and is commonly utilized in coating and advanced manufacturing applications. Both types play crucial roles in enhancing the performance and durability of photovoltaic systems.</p></p>
