<p><p>The electric drill market is primarily divided into corded and cordless types. Corded drills are powered directly by electricity, providing unlimited runtime and consistent power, making them ideal for heavy-duty tasks. They are typically heavier and may restrict mobility due to the power cord. In contrast, cordless drills are battery-operated, offering greater portability and convenience, allowing users to work in various locations without being tethered to a power source. They are favored for lighter tasks and DIY projects.</p></p>
