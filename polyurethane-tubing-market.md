<p><p>Polyurethane tubing is categorized by hardness levels, measured on the Shore A scale, which indicates flexibility and durability. **Hardness 70 A** offers high flexibility, ideal for applications requiring softness and bendability. **Hardness 80 A** provides a balance of flexibility and strength, suitable for general-purpose use. **Hardness 85 A** is more rigid, offering enhanced wear resistance for demanding environments. **Hardness 90 A** is highly durable, used in high-pressure or abrasive conditions. The **Other** market includes specialized hardness levels tailored for unique industrial or medical applications, catering to specific performance needs.</p></p>
