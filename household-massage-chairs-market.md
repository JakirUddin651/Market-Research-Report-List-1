<p><p>The household massage chair market primarily comprises two types: rail massage chairs and trackless massage chairs. Rail massage chairs feature a fixed track along which the massage mechanism moves, providing consistent pressure along designated body areas. They are often more affordable and simpler in design. In contrast, trackless massage chairs offer a more versatile massage experience with free-moving mechanisms that can replicate a wider range of massage techniques, adapting to individual body contours for a personalized experience. Each type caters to different consumer preferences and needs.</p></p>
