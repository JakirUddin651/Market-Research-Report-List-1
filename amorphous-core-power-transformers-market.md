<p><p>The amorphous core power transformers market is divided into two main types: oil-immersed and dry-type transformers. Oil-immersed transformers utilize a liquid dielectric for insulation and cooling, which enhances thermal performance and offers higher efficiency, making them suitable for high-capacity applications. In contrast, dry-type transformers use air as a cooling medium, making them safer in confined spaces and environmentally friendly, ideal for urban settings and indoor installations. Each type addresses specific operational and environmental needs within the power distribution sector.</p></p>
