<p><p>The fluorocarbon rubber market is segmented into various types based on their monomer composition. Vinylidene fluoride (VD) is known for its excellent thermal and chemical resistance. Hexafluoropropylene (HFP) enhances flexibility and compatibility with other polymers. Tetrafluoroethylene (TFE) provides exceptional weathering resistance and low friction properties. Perfluoromethyl vinyl ether (PMVE) offers superior thermal stability and chemical resistance, making it ideal for specialized applications. Additionally, the "Other" category encompasses various niche fluorocarbon rubber formulations catering to specific industrial needs.</p></p>
