<p><p>Screw capping machines are categorized into three main types: manual, semi-automatic, and automatic. Manual machines require operators to apply and tighten caps by hand, suitable for small-scale production. Semi-automatic machines enhance efficiency by assisting the operator with the capping process, ideal for moderate production levels. Automatic machines fully mechanize the capping operation, allowing for high-speed and consistent output, making them suitable for large-scale manufacturing. Each type caters to different production needs and operational budgets.</p></p>
