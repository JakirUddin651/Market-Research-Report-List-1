<p><ul><li>Under-the-seat Black Boxes</li><li>Dashboard Black Boxes</li><li>Windshield Mounted Black Boxes</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive black boxes, or event data recorders, are categorized into three main types based on their installation locations. Under-the-seat black boxes are discreetly placed beneath the seats, providing hidden data recording capabilities. Dashboard black boxes are mounted directly on the dashboard, easily accessible for monitoring and playback. Windshield mounted black boxes are installed on the front windshield, typically offering a clear view of the road and surroundings while capturing critical data during incidents. Each type serves distinct purposes in vehicle data recording and analysis.</p></p>
