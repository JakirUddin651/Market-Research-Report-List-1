<p><strong>The UV-Curable Coatings Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>UV Base-coat</li><li>UV Mid-coat</li><li>UV Top-coat</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The UV-curable coatings market comprises three primary types: UV base-coat, UV mid-coat, and UV top-coat. UV base-coats serve as the foundational layer, providing adhesion and enhancing durability. The UV mid-coat offers additional thickness and gloss, often improving the overall aesthetic and performance. Finally, UV top-coats provide a protective finish that enhances resistance to scratches, chemicals, and UV light, ensuring longevity. Together, these coatings are crucial for various applications in industries such as automotive, furniture, and electronics.</p></p>
