<p><strong>The Ribbon Fiber Optic Cable Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single-Mode</li><li>Multi- Mode</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The ribbon fiber optic cable market comprises two main types: single-mode and multi-mode cables. Single-mode cables feature a narrow core, allowing only one light mode to propagate, which enables long-distance communication with minimal signal loss. In contrast, multi-mode cables have a larger core, accommodating multiple light modes, making them suitable for shorter distances and higher data rates within data centers and local area networks. Both types serve distinct applications, driven by performance needs and installation environments.</p></p>
