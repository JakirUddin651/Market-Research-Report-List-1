<p><strong>The Pharmaceutical Pump Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Centrifugal Pump</li><li>Positive Displacement Pump</li><li>Jet Pump</li><li>Air Lift Pump</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The pharmaceutical pump market includes various types catering to specific applications. Centrifugal pumps use rotational energy to transport fluids, ideal for high flow rates. Positive displacement pumps move fluid by trapping a fixed volume and forcing it through the discharge, offering accurate dosing. Jet pumps utilize high-pressure fluid jets to create suction, suitable for transferring liquids. Air lift pumps rely on air bubbles to lift fluids, effective for specific processes like handling slurries. Each type is essential for ensuring precise and efficient fluid management in pharmaceuticals.</p></p>
