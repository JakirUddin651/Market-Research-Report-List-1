<p><strong>The Fluorescence Lifetime Imaging Microscopy Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Time-Domain</li><li>Frequency-Domain</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Fluorescence Lifetime Imaging Microscopy (FLIM) comprises two primary market types: Time-Domain and Frequency-Domain. Time-Domain FLIM measures the decay time of fluorescence signals after excitation, providing high temporal resolution for analyzing molecular interactions. In contrast, Frequency-Domain FLIM analyzes the modulation of fluorescence signals at specific frequencies, allowing for faster data acquisition and imaging of dynamic processes. Both techniques enhance our understanding of biological systems, enabling researchers to visualize cellular activities and interactions at a molecular level.</p></p>
