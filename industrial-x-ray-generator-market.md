<p><p>The industrial X-ray generator market is divided into high-frequency and low-frequency X-ray generators. High-frequency X-ray generators operate with higher voltage and frequency, leading to better image quality, reduced radiation exposure, and efficient energy use. They are ideal for intricate inspections and precision tasks. In contrast, low-frequency X-ray generators operate at lower frequencies, resulting in larger, less uniform images. While they are generally cost-effective, they are suited for simpler applications where high resolution is not critical.</p></p>
