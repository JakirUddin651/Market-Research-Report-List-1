<p><p>The catch basin drain market is divided primarily into two types of collection basins: Type 1 and Type 2. Type 1 Collection Basins are designed for standard runoff management, featuring a simple inlet system to collect surface water efficiently. In contrast, Type 2 Collection Basins are more advanced, often incorporating filtration systems to treat the water before it enters the drainage system, making them suitable for areas with higher pollutant loads. Both types serve to prevent flooding and maintain water quality.</p></p>
