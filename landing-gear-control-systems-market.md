<p><p>Landing gear control systems encompass various components essential for aircraft operation. Brake metering valves regulate hydraulic pressure during braking, ensuring optimal performance. Parking brake valves maintain stationary aircraft by preventing wheel rotation when engaged. Actuators play a crucial role in extending and retracting landing gear, providing smooth transitions between airborne and ground modes. Other components may include sensors and electronic control units that enhance system functionality. Collectively, these parts ensure safety, reliability, and efficiency in landing gear operations.</p></p>
