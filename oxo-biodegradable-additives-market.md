<p><p>The Oxo-Biodegradable Additives market can be segmented into two primary types: particles and powder. Particles are often used in film and packaging applications, promoting degradation through oxidation when exposed to heat, light, and oxygen. In contrast, powder additives are integrated into plastics during manufacturing, enhancing biodegradability in various applications such as containers and bags. Both forms play a crucial role in addressing plastic waste, facilitating a more environmentally friendly approach by enabling plastics to break down more rapidly in natural conditions.</p></p>
