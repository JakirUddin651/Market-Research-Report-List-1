<p><p>The industrial glass fabrics market is categorized into various weaving types, including plain weave, leno weave, twill weave, and satin weave. Plain weave offers durability and stability, making it suitable for general-purpose applications. Leno weave, characterized by its unique construction, provides enhanced dimensional stability and is often used for lightweight materials. Twill weave features a diagonal pattern, offering increased strength and flexibility, ideal for more demanding applications. Satin weave, known for its smooth finish, provides excellent drape and a lustrous appearance, suitable for specialized uses.</p></p>
