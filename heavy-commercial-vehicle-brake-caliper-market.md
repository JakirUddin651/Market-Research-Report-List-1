<p><p>The Heavy Commercial Vehicle Brake Caliper market features two primary types: single piston calipers and multi-piston calipers. Single piston calipers utilize a single piston to engage the brake pads, offering simplicity and efficiency, making them suitable for lighter loads. In contrast, multi-piston calipers employ multiple pistons, providing enhanced braking power and resistance to heat, ideal for heavier vehicles under rigorous conditions. Both types effectively ensure safe braking performance, catering to varying vehicle requirements within the heavy commercial sector.</p></p>
