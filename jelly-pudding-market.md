<p><p>The jelly pudding market can be categorized into two main types: semi-solid state jelly pudding and no fixed shape jelly pudding. Semi-solid state jelly pudding has a firm texture that retains its shape when removed from a mold, making it ideal for decorative presentations. In contrast, no fixed shape jelly pudding is more fluid and may be served in cups or bowls, allowing for versatility in serving. Both types cater to diverse consumer preferences and occasions in the dessert industry.</p></p>
