<p><p>The aerospace crack detectors market includes various advanced technologies for detecting flaws in materials. Eddy Current testing uses electromagnetic induction to identify surface cracks in conductive materials. Dielectric testing measures changes in electrical properties to uncover defects in non-conductive materials. Imaging technology employs advanced visual techniques to identify surface irregularities and subsurface flaws. Phased Array Ultrasonic testing utilizes multiple ultrasonic beams to scan and analyze materials, providing detailed images of internal structures and enhancing defect detection accuracy in aerospace components.</p></p>
