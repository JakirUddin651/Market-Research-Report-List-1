<p><ul><li>Natural Ester Transformer Fluid</li><li>Synthetic Ester Transformer Oil</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The ester transformer oils market is divided into two main types: natural ester transformer fluid and synthetic ester transformer oil. Natural ester fluids are derived from renewable vegetable sources, offering excellent biodegradability and environmental benefits, making them suitable for environmentally sensitive areas. Synthetic ester oils, on the other hand, are chemically engineered for high-performance applications, providing superior thermal and oxidation stability, as well as high flash points. Both types enhance the safety and efficiency of electrical transformers while addressing environmental concerns.</p></p>
