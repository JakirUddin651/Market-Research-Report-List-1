<p><p>The proppants market includes three main types: frac sand proppants, resin-coated proppants, and ceramic proppants. Frac sand proppants are natural sand used to enhance oil and gas extraction by keeping fractures open. Resin-coated proppants are coated with resin for improved performance and to reduce dissolution in well conditions. Ceramic proppants are man-made, offering high strength and durability, making them suitable for high-pressure environments. Each type uniquely contributes to the efficiency and productivity of hydraulic fracturing operations.</p></p>
