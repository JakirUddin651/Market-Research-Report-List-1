<p><p>SMD thermopile sensors are compact temperature sensors that utilize thermoelectric principles to detect and measure infrared radiation. These sensors consist of multiple thermocouples connected in series, generating a voltage output proportional to the temperature difference at their junctions. With their small footprint, SMD (Surface Mount Device) design allows for easy integration into various electronic devices, making them ideal for applications in consumer electronics, automotive systems, and industrial equipment.</p><p>The SMD thermopile sensor market is experiencing significant growth, driven by an increasing demand for accurate and reliable temperature measurements in diverse applications.
