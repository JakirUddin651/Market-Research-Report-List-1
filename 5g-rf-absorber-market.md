<p><ul><li>Broadband RF Absorber</li><li>Narrowband RF Absorber</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The 5G RF Absorber market comprises two primary types: broadband and narrowband RF absorbers. Broadband RF absorbers are designed to effectively attenuate a wide range of frequencies, making them suitable for diverse applications such as electromagnetic compatibility (EMC) testing and anechoic chambers. In contrast, narrowband RF absorbers are tailored for specific frequency ranges, providing targeted attenuation for particular applications like radar systems or wireless communication at specific channels, optimizing performance in defined spectral windows.</p></p>
