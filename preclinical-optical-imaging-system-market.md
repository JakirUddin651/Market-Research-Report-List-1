<p><p>The preclinical optical imaging system market encompasses various types, including fluorescence and bioluminescence imaging, along with other methods. Fluorescence imaging utilizes fluorescent markers to visualize biological processes, enabling real-time monitoring of cellular events. Bioluminescence imaging relies on light emitted from biological reactions, often using luciferase enzyme systems for sensitive detection of gene expression and cellular activity. Other methods may include near-infrared and multispectral imaging techniques, enhancing the versatility and application of optical imaging in preclinical research and drug development.</p></p>
