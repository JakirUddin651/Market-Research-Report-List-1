<p><p>The Composite Hydrogen Cylinder market is primarily divided into Type III and Type IV cylinders. Type III cylinders feature an aluminum liner wrapped with composite materials, offering a balance of weight, cost, and strength. In contrast, Type IV cylinders consist of a polymer liner fully wrapped with composite materials, resulting in lighter weight and higher resistance to corrosion. These distinct characteristics make Type III ideal for applications needing moderate durability, while Type IV is preferred for applications demanding maximum efficiency and lower weight.</p></p>
