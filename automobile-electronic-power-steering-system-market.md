<p><p>The automobile electronic power steering (EPS) system market can be categorized into brushed and brushless types. Brushed EPS systems use a traditional brushed motor, which offers a cost-effective solution but may have a shorter lifespan due to brush wear. In contrast, brushless EPS systems utilize a brushless motor, providing higher efficiency, greater reliability, and lower maintenance requirements. The brushless option often leads to improved performance and energy conservation, making it increasingly popular among modern vehicles for its superior operational advantages.</p></p>
