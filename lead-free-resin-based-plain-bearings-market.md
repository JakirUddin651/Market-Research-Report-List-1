<p><ul><li>Radial Sliding Bearing</li><li>Thrust Sliding Bearing</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The lead-free resin-based plain bearings market is characterized by two main types: radial sliding bearings and thrust sliding bearings. Radial sliding bearings are designed to support radial loads and allow for smooth rotation, making them ideal for applications like motors and pumps. Thrust sliding bearings, on the other hand, are engineered to handle axial loads, providing stability in applications such as machinery and conveyor systems. Both types offer environmentally friendly alternatives to traditional lead-based bearings, enhancing safety and sustainability.</p></p>
