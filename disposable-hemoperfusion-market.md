<p><p>The disposable hemoperfusion market comprises primarily two types: charcoal hemoperfusion and certain resins hemoperfusion. Charcoal hemoperfusion utilizes activated charcoal to adsorb toxins and small molecules from the blood, making it effective for treating drug overdoses and poisonings. In contrast, certain resins hemoperfusion employs specialized synthetic resins to selectively remove specific toxins and metabolites, offering targeted therapy for certain conditions. Both types enhance detoxification processes, improving patient outcomes in critical care situations.</p></p>
