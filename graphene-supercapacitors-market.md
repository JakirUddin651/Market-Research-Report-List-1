<p><ul><li>Graphene-only Based Supercapacitors</li><li>Hybrid Graphene Supercapacitors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The graphene supercapacitors market is divided into two main types: graphene-only based supercapacitors and hybrid graphene supercapacitors. Graphene-only based supercapacitors utilize pure graphene materials, offering high conductivity and energy storage capabilities, ideal for applications requiring rapid charging and discharging. Hybrid graphene supercapacitors combine graphene with other materials, such as metal oxides or carbon nanotubes, enhancing energy and power density while retaining lightweight properties. This combination optimizes performance, making hybrid models suitable for a wider range of applications.</p></p>
