<p><strong>The Food Extrusion Equipment Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single-screw Extruder</li><li>Twin-screw Extruder</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The food extrusion equipment market primarily consists of two main types: single-screw extruders and twin-screw extruders. Single-screw extruders use a single rotating screw to process food materials, making them suitable for simpler applications like snacks and pasta. In contrast, twin-screw extruders feature two intermeshing screws, allowing for greater mixing, shear, and versatility in handling diverse formulations, often used for more complex products like textured proteins and pet food. Both types enhance efficiency and product quality in food processing.</p></p>
