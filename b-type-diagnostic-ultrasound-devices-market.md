<p><strong>The B-type Diagnostic Ultrasound Devices Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Fanshaped Scanning</li><li>Linear Scanning</li><li>Mixed Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>B-type diagnostic ultrasound devices are classified into three main market types: fanshaped scanning, linear scanning, and mixed type. Fanshaped scanning utilizes a curved array transducer to produce wide images suitable for abdominal and obstetric examinations. Linear scanning employs a straight transducer, offering high-resolution images ideal for vascular and superficial structures. Mixed type devices combine both technologies to enhance versatility, allowing for various imaging applications in one unit. Each type serves specific diagnostic needs within medical imaging.</p></p>
