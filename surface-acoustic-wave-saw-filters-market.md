<p><p>Surface Acoustic Wave (SAW) filters are essential components in the communication and electronics markets, primarily categorized into transversal filters and resonator filters. Transversal filters utilize multiple delay paths to shape the frequency response, suitable for applications requiring broad bandwidth. Resonator filters, on the other hand, use resonant structures to enhance selectivity and minimize insertion loss, making them ideal for narrowband applications. Both types find significant use in wireless communication, consumer electronics, and other precision applications, enhancing signal integrity.</p></p>
