<p><p>The Ultrasonic Flaw Detector market includes several types of instruments designed for non-destructive testing. Conventional Ultrasonic Flaw Detectors utilize standard pulse-echo techniques to identify flaws in materials. Phased Array Ultrasonic Flaw Detectors employ multiple elements and advanced software to create focused beams, allowing for detailed imaging and analyzing of complex geometries. Time of Flight Diffraction (TOFD) Ultrasonic Flaw Detectors measure the time taken for sound waves to reflect off defects, providing precise sizing of flaws. Each type serves specialized applications in various industries.</p></p>
