<p><p>The heat-exchange equipment market consists of three primary types: shell and tube heat exchangers, plate heat exchangers, and air-cooled heat exchangers. Shell and tube exchangers feature a series of tubes within a shell for efficient heat transfer, making them ideal for high-pressure applications. Plate heat exchangers utilize thin plates to enhance heat transfer efficiency, suitable for lower volume applications. Air-cooled heat exchangers use ambient air to dissipate heat, providing energy savings and reduced water usage, ideal for outdoor installations.</p></p>
