<p><p>The Student RFID Tracking Systems market consists of three primary components: tags, readers, and middleware. Tags are small electronic devices attached to student ID cards or uniforms, enabling real-time tracking. Readers are installed at various checkpoints, capturing data from the tags as students pass by. Middleware processes and integrates this data, providing analytics, reporting, and management tools to schools. Together, these components enhance attendance monitoring, improve security, and streamline administrative processes within educational institutions.</p></p>
