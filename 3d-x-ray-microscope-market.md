<p><ul><li>Transmission X-Ray Microscope</li><li>Scanning X-Ray Microscope</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The 3D X-ray Microscope market is primarily divided into two types: Transmission X-Ray Microscopes and Scanning X-Ray Microscopes. Transmission X-Ray Microscopes utilize transmitted X-rays to create high-resolution images of samples, making them ideal for examining internal structures and materials at a nanometer scale. In contrast, Scanning X-Ray Microscopes employ scanning techniques to target specific areas, providing detailed 3D reconstructions and surface analyses. Both types are crucial for advanced materials research, biological studies, and industrial applications.</p></p>
