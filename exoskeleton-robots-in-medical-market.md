<p><p>Exoskeleton robots in the medical market are categorized into three main types: lower body, upper body, and full body. Lower body exoskeletons assist patients with mobility issues, enabling them to stand and walk. Upper body exoskeletons are designed for rehabilitation, supporting arm movement and enhancing upper limb functioning. Full body exoskeletons integrate both upper and lower functionalities, providing comprehensive support for individuals with severe mobility impairments, facilitating rehabilitation and improving overall quality of life.</p></p>
