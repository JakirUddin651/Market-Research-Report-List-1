<p><strong>The Doppler Sensor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>X-Band</li><li>K-Band</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Doppler sensor market is segmented primarily into X-Band and K-Band types, each serving distinct applications. X-Band sensors operate at frequencies between 8 to 12 GHz, making them ideal for short-range radar and weather monitoring. K-Band sensors, ranging from 18 to 27 GHz, are commonly used in traffic enforcement and automotive collision avoidance systems due to their superior resolution and sensitivity. Both types leverage the Doppler effect for accurate velocity measurements, catering to different industry needs and technologies.</p></p>
