<p><p>The cake and patisserie premix market consists of three primary types: complete mixes, dough-base mixes, and dough concentrates. Complete mixes provide all necessary ingredients for easy preparation, requiring only water or eggs. Dough-base mixes offer a foundation that bakers can customize with additional ingredients, allowing for more versatility. Dough concentrates contain a concentrated mixture of ingredients that require further preparation, providing convenience and consistency. Together, these premixes streamline the baking process while maintaining quality and flavor.</p></p>
