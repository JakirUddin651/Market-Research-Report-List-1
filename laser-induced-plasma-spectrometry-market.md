<p><p>Laser-induced Plasma Spectrometry (LIPS) is an advanced analytical technique that utilizes laser-induced plasma to analyze the elemental composition of materials. This method involves focusing a high-energy laser beam onto a sample surface, creating a plasma that emits light. The emitted light is then analyzed to determine the concentration of various elements in the sample, making LIPS a valuable tool in fields such as materials science, environmental monitoring, and quality control in manufacturing.</p><p>The Laser-induced Plasma Spectrometry Market is experiencing significant growth, driven by increasing demand for precise and rapid elemental analysis across multiple industries.
