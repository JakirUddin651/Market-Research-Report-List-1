<p><ul><li>Anti-Helical Fold Manipulation</li><li>Conchal Alteration</li><li>Correction Of Earlobe Prominence</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The otoplasty market encompasses various techniques aimed at enhancing ear aesthetics. Anti-helix fold manipulation involves reshaping the ear's outer structure to create a more defined fold, leading to a more proportionate appearance. Conchal alteration focuses on modifying the concha, the bowl-shaped area of the ear, to reduce its prominence or size. Correction of earlobe prominence addresses earlobes that are overly large or protruding, ensuring a balanced ear shape. These approaches cater to diverse patient needs, contributing to the overall growth of the otoplasty market.</p></p>
