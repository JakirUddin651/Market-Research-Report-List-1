<p><ul><li>Phyto Squalane</li><li>Shark Squalane</li><li>Synthetic Squalane</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The squalane oil market is segmented into three main types: phyto squalane, shark squalane, and synthetic squalane. Phyto squalane is derived from plant sources, primarily olives or sugarcane, and is popular for its sustainability and skin compatibility. Shark squalane, obtained from shark liver oil, is less commonly used due to ethical concerns and environmentally harmful practices. Synthetic squalane, created through hydrogenation of plant-based squalene, offers a cruelty-free and stable alternative, increasingly favored in cosmetics and skincare formulations.</p></p>
