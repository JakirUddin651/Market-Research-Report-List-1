<p><p>The Suspended Solids Monitor market features key types: submersible probes and insert probes. Submersible probes are designed to be fully submerged in liquids, providing real-time measurements of suspended solids levels, ideal for wastewater treatment and environmental monitoring. In contrast, insert probes are installed into the process flow without needing to submerge completely, allowing for easier maintenance and less disruption. Both types play crucial roles in various industries, ensuring compliance with regulations and enhancing operational efficiency.</p></p>
