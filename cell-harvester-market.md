<p><ul><li>Displacement collector</li><li>Therapy collector</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Cell Harvester market is divided into two main types: displacement collectors and therapy collectors. Displacement collectors focus on efficiently isolating cells from various biological samples, often used in research and diagnostics. They employ techniques like centrifugation to separate cells based on density. In contrast, therapy collectors are designed for therapeutic applications, specifically targeting and collecting cells for treatments, such as regenerative medicine or immunotherapies. Both types are essential for advancing medical research and clinical practices.</p></p>
