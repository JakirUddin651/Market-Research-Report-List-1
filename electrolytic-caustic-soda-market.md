<p><strong>The Electrolytic Caustic Soda Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Mercury Process</li><li>Diaphragm Process</li><li>Ion-Exchange Membrane Process</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The electrolytic caustic soda market is primarily categorized into three processes: the Mercury Process, Diaphragm Process, and Ion-Exchange Membrane Process. The Mercury Process uses mercury to separate chlorine, producing a high purity caustic soda but raises environmental concerns. The Diaphragm Process employs a porous diaphragm to separate the products, offering lower capital costs but reduced purity. The Ion-Exchange Membrane Process utilizes selective ion exchange membranes, resulting in high purity and energy efficiency, making it increasingly popular due to its environmental advantages.</p></p>
