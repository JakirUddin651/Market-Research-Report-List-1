<p><p>The Green Steel market consists of two main production types: Electric Arc Furnace (EAF) and Molten Oxide Electrolysis (MOE). EAF technology utilizes recycled steel scrap and renewable energy sources to minimize carbon emissions, making it a more sustainable option. Meanwhile, MOE employs electrolysis to convert iron ore into steel using electricity, eliminating traditional carbon-intensive processes. Both methods aim to reduce the environmental impact of steel production, aligning with global sustainability goals and the demand for greener industrial practices.</p></p>
