<p><h1>Ball Valve Market Insights, Market Players and Forecast Till 2031</h1></p><p><strong>Ball Valve Market Analysis and Latest Trends</strong></p>
<p><p>A ball valve is a type of quarter-turn valve that uses a spherical disc to control the flow of fluid. The ball has a hole through its center, which allows fluid to pass when aligned with the flow path and blocks it when turned 90 degrees. This design offers minimal resistance and quick operation, making ball valves ideal for a variety of applications, including water supply, gas, and various industrial processes.</p><p>The Ball Valve Market is experiencing significant growth, driven by increasing demand in sectors like oil and gas, water treatment, and power generation.
