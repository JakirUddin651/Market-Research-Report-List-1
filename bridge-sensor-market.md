<p><p>The bridge sensor market comprises various configurations, including quarter-bridge, half-bridge, full-bridge, and others. Quarter-bridge setups use one active strain gauge, ideal for simple measurements. Half-bridge employs two gauges for enhanced accuracy and temperature compensation. Full-bridge configurations integrate four gauges, offering maximum sensitivity and best performance in complex applications. Other types may include alternative sensor designs, tailored for specific needs in various industries. Each configuration serves distinct measurement requirements, influencing their market demand and applications.</p></p>
