<p><p>PTFE tapes are categorized into four primary market types based on their density: low density, standard density, medium density, and high density. Low density tapes are flexible and suitable for applications requiring minimal sealing pressure. Standard density tapes offer a balanced performance for general sealing needs. Medium density tapes provide enhanced durability and resistance, making them ideal for more demanding applications. High density tapes are the most robust, designed for extreme conditions requiring maximum sealing efficiency and chemical resistance.</p></p>
