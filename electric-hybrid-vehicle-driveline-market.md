<p><strong>The Electric & Hybrid Vehicle Driveline Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Series Driveline</li><li>Parallel Driveline</li><li>Power Split Driveline</li><li>Electric Driveline</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Electric & Hybrid Vehicle Driveline Market comprises several types: </p><p>1. **Series Driveline**: In this system, the internal combustion engine (ICE) generates electricity to power an electric motor, which drives the wheels.</p><p>2. **Parallel Driveline**: Both the ICE and electric motor can independently provide power to the wheels, enhancing efficiency.</p><p>3. **Power Split Driveline**: Combines features of both series and parallel, allowing power from both sources to be distributed as needed.</p><p>4. **Electric Driveline**: Exclusively uses electric motors for propulsion, resulting in lower emissions.</p><p>5. **Others**: This category includes emerging and specialized driveline configurations.</p></p>
