<p><p>The mechanical linear actuators market includes various types such as screw actuators, cam actuators, and wheel and axle actuators. Screw actuators utilize threaded components to convert rotational motion into linear movement, providing precise control. Cam actuators use a rotating cam profile to achieve linear motion with varying speeds and force. Wheel and axle actuators leverage rotational movement to move loads along a linear path, offering simplicity and efficiency. Each type serves distinct applications across industries, emphasizing reliability and performance.</p></p>
