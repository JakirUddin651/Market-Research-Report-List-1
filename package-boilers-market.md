<p><strong>The Package Boilers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Fire-tube Package Boilers</li><li>Water-tube Package Boilers</li><li>Electric Package Boilers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The package boilers market consists of three main types: fire-tube, water-tube, and electric package boilers. Fire-tube package boilers are designed with the combustion gases traveling through tubes, offering compactness and ease of maintenance, ideal for small-scale applications. Water-tube package boilers, on the other hand, circulate water through tubes heated externally, providing higher pressure and efficiency, suitable for larger industrial uses. Electric package boilers utilize electrical heating elements, making them environmentally friendly for regions with limited fuel availability or emissions restrictions.</p></p>
