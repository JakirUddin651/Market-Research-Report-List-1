<p><ul><li>Visual SLAM</li><li>Laser SLAM</li></ul></p>
<p><p>SLAM (Simultaneous Localization and Mapping) technology is categorized into Visual SLAM and Laser SLAM. Visual SLAM utilizes camera imagery to create maps and identify locations, making it effective for indoor and outdoor environments. It's often applied in robotics and augmented reality applications. In contrast, Laser SLAM employs laser range finders to measure distances, providing precise spatial data, ideal for complex navigation tasks in diverse settings. Both types play crucial roles in autonomous systems, enhancing their mapping and localization capabilities.</p></p>
