<p><ul><li>Full-automatic</li><li>Semi-automatic</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Logistics picking robots are categorized into two main types: full-automatic and semi-automatic. Full-automatic robots operate independently, performing tasks such as picking, packing, and transporting items without human intervention. They are ideal for high-volume warehouses and offer enhanced efficiency. In contrast, semi-automatic robots assist human operators by automating certain tasks while requiring human oversight for other functions. This hybrid approach allows for flexibility and can be more cost-effective for facilities with varying workloads. Both types improve operational efficiency in logistics.</p></p>
