<p><p>The sticky mat market is primarily divided into two types: polyethylene-made and water glue-made mats. Polyethylene mats are constructed from durable plastic, providing a long-lasting and effective barrier against dirt and contaminants, making them ideal for industrial and cleanroom environments. In contrast, water glue mats utilize a sticky adhesive surface that captures particles and debris, offering high tackiness for easy removal and replacement. Each type serves specific needs, catering to various industries such as healthcare, manufacturing, and laboratories.</p></p>
