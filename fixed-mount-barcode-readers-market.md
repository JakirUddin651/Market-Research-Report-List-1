<p><ul><li>Laser Scanner</li><li>Linear Imager</li><li>2D Imager</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The fixed-mount barcode readers market includes three main types: Laser Scanners, Linear Imagers, and 2D Imagers. Laser Scanners utilize laser beams for high-precision reading of 1D barcodes, ideal for fast-paced environments. Linear Imagers capture images of barcodes using a single line of light, effective for reading 1D codes and some low-resolution 2D codes. In contrast, 2D Imagers can read both 1D and 2D barcodes, making them versatile for various applications requiring enhanced data capture and speed.</p></p>
