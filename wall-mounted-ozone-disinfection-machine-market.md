Glass Medium devices utilize glass components to produce ozone for disinfection, providing high durability and stability. In contrast, Non-Glass Dielectric Medium machines use alternative materials like ceramics or plastics, offering flexibility and often lower costs. Both types effectively generate ozone to eliminate pathogens, with varying efficiencies, operational parameters, and suitability for different environments.</p></p>
