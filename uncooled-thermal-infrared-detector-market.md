<p><strong>The Uncooled Thermal Infrared Detector Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Microbolometer IR Detector</li><li>Thermopile IR Detector</li><li>Pyroelectric IR Detector</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The uncooled thermal infrared detector market includes three main types: microbolometer, thermopile, and pyroelectric IR detectors. Microbolometer detectors, made of materials like vanadium oxide, offer high sensitivity for thermal imaging applications. Thermopile detectors convert thermal energy into electrical voltage, suitable for non-contact temperature measurements. Pyroelectric detectors, responsive to thermal changes, are commonly used in motion detection and gas sensing. Each type serves distinct applications, driving advancements in areas such as surveillance, automotive, and medical diagnostics.</p></p>
