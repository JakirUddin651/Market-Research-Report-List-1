<p><p>Low Nitrogen Oxide (NOx) burners are advanced combustion technologies designed to minimize the emission of nitrogen oxides, which are harmful pollutants contributing to smog and acid rain. These burners achieve lower NOx emissions through various methods, including staged combustion, air modulation, and the use of flue gas recirculation. With stringent environmental regulations and growing awareness regarding air quality, the demand for NOx burners has surged across various industries, including power generation, manufacturing, and waste incineration.</p><p>The Low Nitrogen Oxide (NOx) Burners Market is anticipated to grow at a CAGR of 5.6% during the forecast period.
