<p><strong>The Underfill Adhesives Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single Component Underfill Adhesives</li><li>Two-Component Underfill Adhesives</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The underfill adhesives market comprises two primary types: single-component and two-component underfill adhesives. Single-component underfill adhesives are pre-mixed formulations that require no additional components, simplifying the application process and reducing handling time. In contrast, two-component underfill adhesives consist of a resin and a hardener, which must be mixed prior to use, offering enhanced performance characteristics and thermal stability. Both types serve essential roles in semiconductor packaging, providing mechanical support and protecting against moisture and thermal stresses.</p></p>
