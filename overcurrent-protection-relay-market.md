<p><ul><li>Instantaneous Overcurrent Relay</li><li>Definite Time Overcurrent Relay</li><li>Inverse Time Overcurrent Relay</li><li>Directional Overcurrent Relay</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The overcurrent protection relay market consists of several types, including Instantaneous Overcurrent Relays, which react immediately to overcurrent situations; Definite Time Overcurrent Relays, which provide a predetermined delay before tripping; Inverse Time Overcurrent Relays, where the tripping time decreases as the current increases; and Directional Overcurrent Relays, which provide protection based on the current's direction. Each type serves specific applications in power systems, ensuring safety and reliability by preventing damage from excessive currents.</p></p>
