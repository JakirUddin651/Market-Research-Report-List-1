<p><p>Cone Beam Computed Tomography (CBCT) scanners are advanced imaging devices that generate three-dimensional images of dental and maxillofacial structures. They utilize a cone-shaped X-ray beam, capturing multiple images around a single rotation, which are then reconstructed to provide high-resolution, detailed views. CBCT technology is increasingly favored for its ability to enhance diagnostic accuracy, facilitate treatment planning, and improve patient outcomes in fields such as dentistry, orthodontics, and oral surgery.</p><p>The Cone Beam Computed Tomography Scanners Market is experiencing significant growth, with projections estimating a compound annual growth rate (CAGR) of 8.8% during the forecast period. This growth can be attributed to rising demand for minimally invasive diagnostic procedures, advancements in imaging technology, and increasing awareness among healthcare professionals about the benefits of CBCT in treatment planning. Additionally, the growing prevalence of dental diseases and disorders is further propelling market expansion. Recent trends indicate a shift towards portable and compact CBCT systems, catering to the needs of smaller clinics and boosting accessibility. Furthermore, integration with digital workflows and ongoing innovations in imaging software are enhancing the capabilities of CBCT systems, driving their adoption across healthcare settings.</p></p>
