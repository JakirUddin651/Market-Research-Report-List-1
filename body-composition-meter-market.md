<p><p>The body composition meter market comprises two primary types: digital and electric. Digital body composition meters utilize advanced sensors and algorithms to provide precise measurements of body fat, muscle mass, and other metrics, often displaying results on an easy-to-read screen. Electric body composition meters, on the other hand, typically employ bioelectrical impedance analysis to estimate body composition, relying on electrical signals to assess resistance within body tissues. Both types cater to health-conscious consumers, fitness enthusiasts, and professionals monitoring physical health.</p></p>
