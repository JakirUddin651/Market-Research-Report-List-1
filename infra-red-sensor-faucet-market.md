<p><p>The infrared sensor faucet market is categorized into two main types: Alternating Current (AC) sensor faucets and Direct Current (DC) sensor faucets. AC sensor faucets are powered by standard electrical outlets, providing consistent performance and often featuring more advanced functionalities. In contrast, DC sensor faucets operate on battery power, making them more energy-efficient and easier to install in locations without direct power supply. Both types enhance hygiene and convenience by facilitating touchless operation in various public and private settings.</p></p>
