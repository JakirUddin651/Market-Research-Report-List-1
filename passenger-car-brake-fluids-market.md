<p><p>The passenger car brake fluids market consists of various types, primarily categorized by DOT ratings. DOT 3 is a glycol-based fluid, suitable for standard vehicles with lower performance requirements. DOT 4 offers higher boiling points, making it ideal for vehicles subjected to more intense braking conditions. DOT 5 is a silicone-based fluid, designed for specialized applications and not typically used in standard passenger cars due to its incompatibility with other fluids. Each type caters to specific performance and safety needs.</p></p>
