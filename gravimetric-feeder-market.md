<p><ul><li>Single Screw Gravimetric Feeder</li><li>Twin Screw Gravimetric Feeder</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The gravimetric feeder market is segmented into three main types: Single Screw Gravimetric Feeders, Twin Screw Gravimetric Feeders, and Others. Single screw feeders utilize one auger for precise volumetric measurement, ideal for simple bulk materials. Twin screw feeders enhance accuracy and variability by using two screws, accommodating diverse materials and providing better flow control. The "Others" category includes specialized feeders, such as vibratory or belt-based systems, catering to unique application requirements in various industries, including pharmaceuticals, food, and plastics.</p></p>
