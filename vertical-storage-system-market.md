<p><ul><li>Vertical Lift Module (VLM)</li><li>Vertical Carousel</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The vertical storage system market includes two primary types: Vertical Lift Modules (VLMs) and Vertical Carousels. VLMs consist of stackable trays that move vertically between input and output ports, allowing for efficient storage and retrieval of items, ideal for varying item sizes. Vertical Carousels, on the other hand, use rotating shelves to deliver items to the operator, optimizing space in warehouses. Both systems enhance inventory management, reduce retrieval times, and maximize storage capacity in compact footprints.</p></p>
