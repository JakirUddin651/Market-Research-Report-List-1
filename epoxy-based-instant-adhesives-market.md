<p><p>Epoxy-based instant adhesives are categorized into cold cure and heat cure adhesives. Cold cure adhesives are designed to bond at room temperature, making them convenient for a variety of quick repair applications. They offer strong adhesion without the need for external heat sources. In contrast, heat cure adhesives require elevated temperatures to achieve optimal bonding strength, typically enhancing performance in industrial settings. Both types cater to diverse industries, including automotive, aerospace, and electronics, addressing specific bonding needs and environmental conditions.</p></p>
