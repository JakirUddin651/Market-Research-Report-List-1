Coconut shell activated carbon filters are known for their high adsorption capacity and are effective in removing volatile organic compounds and odors. Nutshell activated carbon filters, derived from various nut shells, offer good filtration properties and are typically more cost-effective. Wood activated carbon filters come from hardwood sources and are often used for general air and water purification due to their effectiveness and affordability.</p></p>
