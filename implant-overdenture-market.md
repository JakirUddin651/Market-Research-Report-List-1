<p><ul><li>Tooth-Supported</li><li>Implant-Supported</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The implant overdenture market consists of two primary types: tooth-supported and implant-supported overdentures. Tooth-supported overdentures rely on existing natural teeth for support, providing a more stable fit while preserving some natural dentition. In contrast, implant-supported overdentures utilize dental implants placed in the jawbone to anchor the denture securely, offering enhanced stability and function. Both options cater to patients with varying levels of bone density and preferences, allowing for improved oral function and quality of life.</p></p>
