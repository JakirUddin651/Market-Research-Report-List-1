<p><p>The GNSS boards market is categorized into single-frequency and multi-frequency types. Single-frequency GNSS boards operate using a single satellite signal, suited for applications requiring basic positioning accuracy. In contrast, multi-frequency GNSS boards utilize multiple signals simultaneously, enhancing precision and reliability by mitigating errors caused by atmospheric conditions. Multi-frequency systems are preferred for demanding applications like autonomous vehicles and surveying, where high accuracy is essential. The growing need for precise navigation drives the demand for both market types.</p></p>
