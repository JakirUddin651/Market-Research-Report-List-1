<p><ul><li>Current Control Technology</li><li>Temperature Control Technology</li><li>Mechanical Control Technology</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The tunable lasers market is segmented into three main types based on control technologies. Current control technology involves modulating the electrical current to achieve precise wavelength adjustments. Temperature control technology utilizes thermal variations to tune the laser output, enhancing wavelength stability and performance. Mechanical control technology incorporates physical methods, such as adjusting mirror positions or cavity lengths, to fine-tune the laser's emission wavelength. Each technology caters to varying applications across telecommunications, spectroscopy, and defense, emphasizing flexibility and precision.</p></p>
