<p><p>The solid milling cutters market comprises various types, primarily categorized by their unique cutting edges and structural components. Peripheral cutting edge cutters are designed for horizontal machining, effectively removing material along the workpiece's periphery. End cutting edge cutters, on the other hand, engage the workpiece's surface, suitable for vertical milling operations. The shank provides the necessary toolholder connection, while the neck parts, often tapered, facilitate access to tight spaces. Together, these components enhance the versatility and efficiency of milling operations.</p></p>
