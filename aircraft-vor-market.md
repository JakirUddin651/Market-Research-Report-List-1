<p><p>The Aircraft VOR (VHF Omnidirectional Range) market includes two primary display types: Analog and Digital. Analog Display Type uses traditional mechanical gauges with rotating needles to indicate VOR navigation data, offering simplicity and reliability but limited precision. Digital Display Type employs electronic screens to present VOR information, providing enhanced accuracy, clarity, and integration with modern avionics systems. Both types serve the same purpose of aiding aircraft navigation, but digital displays are increasingly favored for their advanced functionality and compatibility with contemporary cockpit technologies.</p></p>
