<p><strong>The Chlor-alkali Equipment Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Ion Exchange Membrane Method</li><li>Diaphragm Method</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The chlor-alkali equipment market primarily includes two methods for producing chlorine and alkali: the Ion Exchange Membrane Method and the Diaphragm Method. The Ion Exchange Membrane Method offers higher efficiency and purity, utilizing a selective membrane to separate products while reducing energy consumption. In contrast, the Diaphragm Method employs a porous diaphragm to separate chlorine and caustic soda but is generally less efficient and may produce lower purity products. Both methods cater to different industrial needs and environmental regulations.</p></p>
