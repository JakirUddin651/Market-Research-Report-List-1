<p><strong>The Thyristor Controlled Series Capacitor (TCSC) Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Blocking Mode</li><li>Bypass Mode</li><li>Capacitive Boost Mode (Capacitive Vernier Mode)</li><li>Inductive Boost Mode (Inductive Vernier Mode)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Thyristor Controlled Series Capacitor (TCSC) enhances transmission stability and reduces losses in power systems. The market is categorized into four modes: </p><p>1. **Blocking Mode**: The thyristors are off, allowing no current flow, stabilizing the system.</p><p>2. **Bypass Mode**: The TCSC bypasses the capacitor, preventing it from affecting the system during disturbances.</p><p>3. **Capacitive Boost Mode**: It injects additional capacitive reactance to improve voltage and reduce transmission line congestion.</p><p>4. **Inductive Boost Mode**: It provides inductive reactance, helping in voltage control and system stability by counteracting over-voltages.</p></p>
