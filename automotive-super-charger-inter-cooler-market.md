<p><p>The automotive supercharger intercooler market consists of different types, primarily including air-to-air and air-to-liquid systems, along with others. Air-to-liquid systems, on the other hand, use a liquid coolant to absorb heat, offering improved cooling performance and compactness, often found in high-performance applications.
