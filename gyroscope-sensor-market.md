<p><p>The gyroscope sensor market encompasses various types, including infrared, ultrasonic, microwave, and others. Infrared gyroscopes utilize infrared light to detect angular motion, offering high precision in compact applications. Ultrasonic gyroscopes employ sound waves to measure rotation, suitable for environments where light-based systems may fail. Microwave gyroscopes leverage microwave signals for accurate angular position detection, often used in aerospace applications. Other types may include mechanical and fiber-optic gyroscopes, each designed for specific use cases across different industries.</p></p>
