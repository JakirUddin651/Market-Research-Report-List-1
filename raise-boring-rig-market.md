<p><strong>The Raise Boring Rig Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Raise-boring</li><li>Down-reaming</li><li>Box-holing</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Raise Boring Rig Market consists of three primary types: raise-boring, down-reaming, and box-holing. Raise-boring involves creating vertical or inclined shafts by boring from the bottom up, making it efficient for mining applications. Down-reaming increases the diameter of existing holes, enhancing ventilation or access. Box-holing, often used in tunneling, involves creating cross-sectional openings through a series of precise boring operations. Each method caters to specific operational needs, optimizing excavation and operational efficiency in various mining and construction environments.</p></p>
