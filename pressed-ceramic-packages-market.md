<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Pressed Ceramic Packages Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Ceramic-metal Sealing (CERTM)</li><li>Glass-Metal Sealing (GTMS)</li><li>Passivation Glass</li><li>Transponder Glass</li><li>Reed Glass</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Pressed ceramic packages encompass various types of sealing and materials. Ceramic-metal sealing (CERTM) combines ceramic and metal for robust, high-temperature applications. Glass-metal sealing (GTMS) utilizes glass to form a reliable bond between the glass and metal components, ensuring durability. Passivation glass provides a protective layer to enhance electronic performance, while transponder glass encapsulates electronic components for secure communication. Reed glass serves as a medium for magnetic switches, offering reliable performance in various electronic applications, highlighting the versatility of ceramic packaging solutions.</p></p>
