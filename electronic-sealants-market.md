<p><p>The electronic sealants market is categorized into three main types: light curing, thermal curing, and others. Light curing sealants use ultraviolet or visible light to initiate a rapid curing process, offering quick application and strong adhesion. Thermal curing sealants rely on heat to activate their curing mechanism, providing durability and resistance to extreme conditions. Other sealants include those that cure through chemical reactions or moisture exposure, catering to various applications in electronics, automotive, and construction industries.</p></p>
