<p><ul><li>Water-Based Cutting Fluids</li><li>Cutting Oils</li><li>Emulsions</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The metal cutting fluid and oil market comprises three main types: water-based cutting fluids, cutting oils, and emulsions. Water-based cutting fluids are primarily composed of water and additives, providing excellent cooling and lubrication, while being environmentally friendly. Cutting oils are generally mineral or synthetic oils offering superior lubrication for high-performance machining. Emulsions blend oil and water, combining the benefits of both, offering effective cooling and lubrication for a wide range of machining applications, balancing performance and cost.</p></p>
