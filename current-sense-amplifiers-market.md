<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Current Sense Amplifiers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>High-side Sensing</li><li>Low-side Sensing</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Current Sense Amplifier (CSA) market consists of two primary types: high-side sensing and low-side sensing. High-side sensing measures current flowing to the load, providing better safety and accuracy by isolating the measurement from ground potential. Conversely, low-side sensing measures current returning from the load, typically offering simpler design but potentially exposing the sensing circuit to noise and ground disturbances. Both types serve critical roles in various applications, including power management, industrial automation, and electric vehicles, driving their market demand.</p></p>
