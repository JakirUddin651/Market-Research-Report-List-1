<p><ul><li>Spunlace Technology</li><li>Airlaid Technology</li><li>Wetlaid Technology</li><li>Spunlaid Technology</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The wet tissue and wipe market encompasses several technologies for producing nonwoven materials. Spunlace technology involves bonding fibers using high-pressure water jets, resulting in soft, absorbent wipes. Airlaid technology uses air to disperse fibers, creating a lightweight and absorbent product suitable for various applications. Wetlaid technology relies on water to form a web of fibers, allowing for excellent strength and uniformity. Spunlaid technology combines synthetic fibers through spinning and bonding, providing durability and versatility for wipes and tissues.</p></p>
