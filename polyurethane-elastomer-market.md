<p><strong>The Polyurethane Elastomer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Thermoset PU Elastomers</li><li>Thermoplastic PU Elastomers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The polyurethane elastomer market is categorized into thermoset and thermoplastic types. Thermoset polyurethane (PU) elastomers are characterized by their permanent, cross-linked structures that provide excellent durability, chemical resistance, and thermal stability, making them ideal for industrial applications. In contrast, thermoplastic polyurethane elastomers (TPU) can be reshaped upon heating and offer flexibility, ease of processing, and recyclability. TPUs are widely used in various sectors, including automotive, footwear, and consumer goods, due to their versatility and performance.</p></p>
