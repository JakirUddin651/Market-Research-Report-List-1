<p><p>The desktop air purifier market encompasses three main types: passive, active, and hybrid. Passive purifiers rely on natural airflow and are often designed with filters that capture pollutants without mechanical assistance. Active purifiers utilize technologies like ionization or UV light to actively purify the air, often offering faster results. Hybrid devices combine both passive filtration and active purification methods, maximizing efficiency and effectiveness in air cleaning. Each type caters to different consumer needs and preferences, enhancing indoor air quality in various settings.</p></p>
