<p><p>The local cryotherapy machines market can be categorized into three main types: Liquid Nitrogen Systems, Electric Systems, and Others. Liquid Nitrogen Systems utilize cryogenic gases to deliver extremely low temperatures, effective for targeted treatments. Electric Systems use refrigeration techniques to achieve cooling without the need for gases, offering convenience and portability. The “Others” category encompasses alternative technologies and hybrids that combine various methods, catering to diverse therapeutic applications and user preferences, enhancing accessibility in both clinical and personal settings.</p></p>
