<p><ul><li>Filter Type</li><li>Valve Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The medical sterilization containers market is categorized into two main types: filter type and valve type. Filter type containers utilize filtration systems to allow steam or gas penetration while preventing contaminants from entering, ensuring effective sterilization. Valve type containers feature built-in valves that control the flow of sterilizing agents and release gases post-sterilization, maintaining the integrity of the sterile environment. Both types aim to enhance safety, efficiency, and reliability in the sterilization process of medical instruments.</p></p>
