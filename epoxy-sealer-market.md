<p><ul><li>Water Based</li><li>Solvent Based</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The epoxy sealer market is categorized into two main types: water-based and solvent-based. Water-based epoxy sealers are environmentally friendly, offering low VOC emissions and easy cleanup, making them ideal for indoor applications. They provide a durable finish and are suitable for various surfaces. In contrast, solvent-based epoxy sealers contain higher VOC levels, offering superior adhesion and chemical resistance, making them ideal for heavy-duty applications, such as industrial floors and outdoor settings. Each type serves distinct needs in construction and maintenance.</p></p>
