<p><p>The SiC MOSFET module market is categorized into two main types: Full Silicon Carbide Modules and Hybrid Silicon Carbide Modules. Full Silicon Carbide Modules utilize SiC components exclusively, offering higher efficiency and thermal performance, making them ideal for demanding applications. In contrast, Hybrid Silicon Carbide Modules combine SiC with traditional silicon components, providing a cost-effective solution while enhancing performance compared to conventional silicon-only modules. These classifications cater to different performance needs, application environments, and budget considerations.</p></p>
