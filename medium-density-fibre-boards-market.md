<p><p>Medium Density Fibre Boards (MDF) are categorized into two main market types: Type I and Type II. Type I MDF is primarily designed for interior applications and is known for its smooth surface, making it ideal for furniture, cabinetry, and decorative panels. Type II MDF, on the other hand, is engineered for exterior use, featuring enhanced moisture resistance, making it suitable for applications such as outdoor furniture and construction elements exposed to humidity. Each type meets specific durability and performance requirements.</p></p>
