<p><p>The Ceramic Conductive Silver Paste market is divided into two primary types based on temperature application: low-temperature silver paste and high-temperature silver paste. Low-temperature silver paste is designed for applications that require firing at lower temperatures, making it suitable for sensitive substrates like plastics or ceramics. In contrast, high-temperature silver paste is engineered to withstand elevated firing temperatures, ideal for robust applications in electronics and automotive industries. Both types are essential for enhancing conductivity in various ceramic components.</p></p>
