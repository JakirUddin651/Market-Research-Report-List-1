<p><p>Box sealing machines are vital in packaging and logistics, primarily categorized into fully automatic and semi-automatic carton sealers. Fully automatic carton sealers streamline the sealing process by automatically adjusting to various box sizes, applying adhesive tape or glue with minimal human intervention. Conversely, semi-automatic carton sealers require some manual input, where operators align boxes for sealing. Both types enhance efficiency and consistency, reducing packaging time and labor costs while ensuring secure closure for products during storage and transportation.</p></p>
