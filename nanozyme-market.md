<p><p>The nanozyme market comprises four key types: </p><p>1. **Active Metal Centre Mimic** - These nanozymes replicate the behavior of natural enzymes, focusing on metal active sites for catalytic activities.</p><p>  </p><p>2. **Functional Mimic** - Designed to imitate the overall function of enzymes, these nanozymes facilitate biochemical reactions without necessarily mirroring structural features. </p><p>3. **Nanocomposites** - These combine nanoparticles with other materials to enhance catalytic performance, stability, and specificity.</p><p>4. **3D Structural Mimic** - These nanozymes replicate the three-dimensional structures of natural enzymes, enabling specific interactions and improving reaction efficiency.</p></p>
