<p><strong>The Oil Skimmers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Oleophilic Skimmers</li><li>Non-Oleophilic Skimmers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The oil skimmers market is primarily divided into two types: oleophilic and non-oleophilic skimmers. Oleophilic skimmers are designed to attract and absorb oil, utilizing materials that preferentially bind to oil over water, making them effective for capturing floating oil on water surfaces. In contrast, non-oleophilic skimmers rely on mechanical methods to separate oil from water, often using weirs or other physical barriers. Both types are essential for oil spill recovery, wastewater treatment, and environmental clean-up efforts.</p></p>
