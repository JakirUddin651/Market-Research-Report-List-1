<p><strong>The Smoke Alarm (Smoke Detector) Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Photoelectric</li><li>Ionization</li><li>Dual Sensor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The smoke alarm market comprises three primary types: photoelectric, ionization, and dual sensor alarms. Photoelectric alarms use a light source to detect smoke particles, making them effective for smoldering fires. Ionization alarms utilize radioactive material to sense flames, excelling in detecting fast-flaming fires. Dual sensor alarms combine both technologies, providing comprehensive protection against various fire types. Each type offers distinct advantages, catering to diverse safety needs in residential, commercial, and industrial environments, ultimately enhancing fire safety and response times.</p></p>
