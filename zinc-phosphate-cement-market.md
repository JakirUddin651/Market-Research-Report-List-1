<p><p>The Zinc Phosphate Cement market is categorized into three main types: powder, liquid, and set. The powder component contains zinc oxide and magnesium oxide, providing the base for the cement. The liquid part typically consists of phosphoric acid, which, when mixed with the powder, initiates the hardening process. The set market refers to the final product, which has hardened and attained its desired properties. Together, these components create a durable and widely used dental and construction adhesive.</p></p>
