<p><p>Fingertip pulse oximetry refers to a non-invasive method used to measure the oxygen saturation level in a person's blood. Its primary application lies in monitoring patients with respiratory issues, during anesthesia, or in critical care settings, making it a crucial tool in healthcare.</p><p>The Fingertip Pulse Oximetry Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 11.3% during the forecast period.
